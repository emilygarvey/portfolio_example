Projects

I am most well-known for developing Voice over IP, which is used for video conference software.

I came up with the idea that allows people to donate to charity by texting. This idea was inspired by American Idol's system of allowing people to vote for their favorite singer via texting.

At Google, I managed the team that launched the technology for Project Loon, which flies balloons over areas to provide them with internet access.

I am the Vice President of Engineering focused on Access Strategy & Emerging Markets. That means I lead oversee teams that use creative ways to bring internet all over the world. One of these programs, Internet Saathi, focuses on getting internet access to women in India.
